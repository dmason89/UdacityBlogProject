How To Beat 6-Max Hypers

Here are a few strategies that can greatly improve your win rate:

Play Tight In Early Rounds
You typically start with a short stack and blinds move fast. Only play a solid range in the first stage so you have chips to shove later on

Know Your ICM
6 max hypers are all about know when to shove/fold. Study ICM and know the right ranges for each position.

Pick Your Battles
Your goal is to profit. When you're on the bubble and you don't have the biggest stack, be cautious. Let the other short stack bust.

Tracking Your Opponents

Why should you track your opponents?

Having as much info as possible on your opponents can only help you. Say you sit down at a new table and your opponents goes all-in the very first hand. Well, with no knowledge on this individual, you may call or fold in a spot you really shouldn't have.

Say we are in the same exact spot, only this time we have looked up our opponent and see they have a -28% roi. You probably will feel a lot more comfortable making the call with your pocket 8's
